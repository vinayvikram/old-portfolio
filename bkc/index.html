About Us

CBI - Chai by 2 IITian's,
A chai start-up which makes you feel special.

BKC’s outlet helps students, Business professionals and elders who all want to boost their mood, need positive energy and a feeling of Trust by providing a more hygienic, consistent and trustworthy environment unlike stalls without a focus on the inner requirements of customers.

Founded by two IITian’s with a mission to provide quick, hygienic, trustworthy, easy and satisfactory products & services at the best possible rates.

In order to build trust among people, founders took the initiative of showing full trust on customers without any expectation in return.

With this thought customers need not to pay everytime when they enjoy services, BKC trusts them and know that they will meet our trust level too.

Benefits of BKC’s initiative-
* Social Cause=Research says that showing unexpected trust results in building trust.
* Easy Transaction = Get rid of daily scan and pay or wallet cash.
* Mental relief = Don’t bother for daily contri’s or everytime paying bills of your  clever friends – Now everyone’s share will credit on their own name.
* Technology Enabled =  Instant bill update of order confirmation on your smartphone, and many more.

BKC's vision is to grow a seed of Trust in all individuals which germinates to an another level and will change the whole surrounding.
